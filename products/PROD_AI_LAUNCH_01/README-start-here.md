# Launch Optimizer (GR-LAUNCH) - Start Here

Ready to launch? This kit provides the strategy, copy, and planning tools to maximize your product release.

## Components
- **Launch-Optimizer-Guide.md**: Strategy overview.
- **Prompts**:
  - `positioning.txt`: Define your USP and audience.
  - `sales-optimization.txt`: Improve conversion on your pages.
  - `launch-sequence.txt`: Generate email/social content schedules.
- **Templates**:
  - `sales-page-outline.md`: Wireframe for high conversion.
  - `email-pack.md`: The essential 5-email sequence.
- **Bonus**: Launch Planner (Notion).

## Quick Start
1. Run `prompts/positioning.txt` to clarify your messaging.
2. Use the output to fill in `templates/sales-page-outline.md`.
3. Generate your email sequence using `prompts/launch-sequence.txt`.
