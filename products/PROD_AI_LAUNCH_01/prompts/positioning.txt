# Role
Act as a Product Marketing Manager.

# Context
I am launching a new product: [PRODUCT NAME].
Brief description: [INSERT DESCRIPTION]

# Task
Help me define the positioning.

# Output Requirements
1. **Target Audience Persona**: Demographics, Psychographics, Pain Points.
2. **Unique Selling Proposition (USP)**: One clear sentence.
3. **The "Before/After" Grid**:
   - Before (Problem State)
   - After (Solution State)
4. **Key Benefits vs. Features**: List 3 top features and translate them into emotional benefits.
