## üìñ How to Use This Pack
This isn't just a collection of prompts; it's a **launch system**. It's designed to take you from a vague idea to a executed launch in 14 days.

### Phase 1: Positioning & Validation (Days 1-3)
*   **Goal:** Ensure people actually want what you're building.
*   **Tool:** Use `prompts/01-positioning-validator.txt`.
*   **Input:** Your rough idea and target audience.
*   **Output:** A refined "Hook," unique value proposition, and a list of "pain points" your product solves.

### Phase 2: The "Perfect" Sales Page (Days 4-7)
*   **Goal:** Convert visitors into buyers.
*   **Tool:** Use `prompts/02-sales-page-builder.txt`.
*   **Input:** The output from Phase 1.
*   **Output:** A section-by-section copy for your Gumroad/Landing page (Hero, Problem, Agitation, Solution, Proof, CTA).

### Phase 3: The Launch Sequence (Days 8-14)
*   **Goal:** Drive urgency and sales.
*   **Tool:** Use `prompts/03-email-sequence-generator.txt`.
*   **Output:** A 5-email sequence (Tease, Announce, Educate, Social Proof, Last Call).
