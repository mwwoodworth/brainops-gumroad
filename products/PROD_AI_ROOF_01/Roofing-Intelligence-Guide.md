## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Success

> **"The AI is your junior estimator, not your boss."**

*   **Sanitize Data:** Never paste client names, phone numbers, or exact addresses into public AI tools. Use "Client X" or "Project Alpha".
*   **Context is King:** The more detail you give the prompt about the *site conditions* (e.g., "urban area, tight clearance, 4th floor"), the better the output.
*   **Iterate:** If the AI output is too generic, tell it: *"Be more specific about TPO membrane installation on a 2/12 pitch."*
