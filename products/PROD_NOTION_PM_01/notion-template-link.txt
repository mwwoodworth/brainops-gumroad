## Database Structure Overview (for reference)

### Projects DB
- Status (Not Started, In Progress, Done)
- Timeline (Date Range)
- Progress (Rollup of Tasks)

### Tasks DB
- Status (Kanban)
- Assignee (Person)
- Due Date (Date)
- Relation -> Projects

### Resources/Docs
- Type (Spec, Contract, Design)
- Link (URL)
